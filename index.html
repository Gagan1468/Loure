Our Sweet Story

From a passion for baking to a beloved patisserie — every cake tells a story of love and craft.

About the Brand

Lueur Patisserie was born from a deep love for the art of baking. The name "Lueur" — meaning a glimmer or soft glow in French — captures our philosophy: that every cake should bring a warm glow of joy to the people who receive it.

We specialize in 100% eggless cakes, customized to perfection for every occasion. From birthdays to weddings, baby showers to corporate celebrations — each creation is a unique edible work of art.

Using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients with zero preservatives, we ensure that every bite is as memorable as the moment it celebrates.
